About Barberscore

My name is David Binetti and I built this app because I wanted a place where I could track scores and information related to each performer during a contest.

Most of the hard work was done by Alexander Boltenko (@RusskibassTampa), who sings bass with the Heralds of Harmony in Florida. He's entered all the data and updates the contest results throughout. Without him this would be an empty, worthless shell.

I will not be using or sharing your personal information in any way and I'm not making any money off of this. I'm just a barbershopper/geek and thought it would be a cool app.

Source code for this app can be found here. If you have any questions or need support open an issue there.
