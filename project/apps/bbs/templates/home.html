Welcome to Barberscore, a simple way to track peformances during the Toronto Convention of the Barbershop Harmony Society.

This free app provides the list of contestants and contest schedule, along with a way to write notes and rate performances on your mobile phone or desktop. Getting the list of contestants is available immediately; rating performances requires a North American mobile phone for login. I am working on a solution for international barbershoppers.

Login Now

I will not be using or sharing your personal information in any way; I'm just a barbershopper and a geek and thought it would be cool to build something for convention.

My name is David Binetti, I've sung with the Harmonizers and Voices in Harmony. Source code for this app can be found here. If you have any questions or need support email me at support@barberscore.zendesk.com.
