Why Mobile?

We Use a Mobile Number to Keep Things Simple and Secure


When you login with your mobile number, we'll send you a four-digit text that you use to access your account. It's much easier than trying to remember endless username/email/password combinations (particuarly for infrequently accessed sites like this one) and it's actually safer because there's no password to be lost or stolen!

We won't share your number or anything like that. It's just a lot easier to handle than doing password resets, and email switching and all that other stuff that you do when you forget logins (which we all do.) If you're overseas or otherwise can't receive texts, then send us an email at support@barberscore.com and we'll try to figure something out.
